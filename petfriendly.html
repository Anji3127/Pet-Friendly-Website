About the Campaign 🐕‍🦺

The Pet-Friendly City Campaign is a community-driven initiative dedicated to making urban spaces more welcoming for our furry friends. 🏙️🐾
From building more dog parks to supporting pet-friendly housing and encouraging responsible pet ownership, we’re here to make cities more joyful—for pets and their people!
